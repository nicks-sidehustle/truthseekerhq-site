TIMELINE OF DISCLOSURE

1947

Roswell Incident

Military initially announces "flying disc" recovery, then changes story to weather balloon. Witnesses report alien bodies. Files still classified.

1952

Washington D.C. UFO Flap

UFOs repeatedly detected over restricted airspace including the White House and Capitol. Jets scrambled. Front-page news nationwide.

1969

Project Blue Book Closed

Air Force ends official UFO investigation. Concludes "no threat" despite 701 cases remaining "unidentified." Many believe real program went underground.

1997

Phoenix Lights

Thousands witness massive V-shaped craft over Arizona. Governor Fife Symington initially mocked it, later admitted he saw it and it was "otherworldly."

2004

USS Nimitz Encounter

Navy pilots encounter and record "Tic Tac" UFO. Video classified until 2017 leak. Multiple pilots and radar operators confirm.

2017

NYT Reveals AATIP

Secret Pentagon UFO program exposed. Videos released. Former program head Luis Elizondo goes public. Disclosure era begins.

2021

UAP Task Force Report

Government officially acknowledges 144 UAP encounters. Only 1 explained. Admits potential national security threat. "UAP" replaces "UFO."

2023

Grusch Congressional Testimony

Whistleblower testifies under oath about crash retrievals, non-human biologics, and illegal cover-up. Congress demands answers. AARO established.
